UC-X Test Results

Q1: Can I carry forward unused annual leave?
A1: Yes. Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year, and any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December. Source: policy_hr_leave.txt section 2.6.

Q2: Can I install Slack on my work laptop?
A2: Slack can be installed on a corporate device only with written approval from the IT Department. Source: policy_it_acceptable_use.txt section 2.3.

Q3: What is the home office equipment allowance?
A3: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance of Rs 8,000. Source: policy_finance_reimbursement.txt section 3.1.

Q4: Can I use my personal phone for work files from home?
A4: No. Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only, and personal devices must not be used to access, store, or transmit classified or sensitive CMC data. Source: policy_it_acceptable_use.txt section 3.1; policy_it_acceptable_use.txt section 3.2.

Q5: What is the company view on flexible working culture?
A5: This question is not covered in the available policy documents (policy_hr_leave.txt, policy_it_acceptable_use.txt, policy_finance_reimbursement.txt). Please contact the relevant department for guidance.

Q6: Can I claim DA and meal receipts on the same day?
A6: No. If actual meal expenses are claimed instead of DA, receipts are mandatory and DA and meal receipts cannot be claimed simultaneously for the same day. Source: policy_finance_reimbursement.txt section 2.6.

Q7: Who approves leave without pay?
A7: Leave Without Pay requires approval from both the Department Head and the HR Director; manager approval alone is not sufficient. Source: policy_hr_leave.txt section 5.2.
